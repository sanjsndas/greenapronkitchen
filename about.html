More than a recipe blog

Green Apron Kitchen was born from a simple belief: cooking should be understood, not just followed. We teach the science, the history, and the intuition behind every dish.

Our philosophy

🌱 Season first

We cook with what’s growing nearby. Our recipes adapt to farmers' markets, not the other way around.

🔬 Technique over trends

Knife skills, emulsion, fermentation — we focus on fundamentals that make every meal better.

📖 Storytelling

Every recipe has a story. We share the origins, the trials, and the happy accidents that led to our best versions.

🤝 Community

We answer every question, celebrate every success, and learn from every failure alongside our readers.

Our journey

2018

Green Apron Kitchen started as a small newsletter for friends. First recipe: sourdough pancakes.

2020

Launched the website with 35 recipes. Gained a following during the home-baking boom.

2022

Added video tutorials and ingredient science deep-dives. Reached 500k monthly readers.

2025

Published our first cookbook (forthcoming). Still posting free recipes every week.

What we stand for

Zero food waste — we design recipes that use every part of the ingredient. From carrot tops to citrus peels.

Realistic cooking — we test every recipe in home kitchens with standard equipment. No sous-vide required.

Inclusivity — our recipes are vegetarian-forward, with easy swaps for dietary needs. We believe good food is for everyone.
