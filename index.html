    Frequently Asked Questions
    Can I substitute whole wheat for all‑purpose?

    Yes, but expect denser results. For best outcome, use 50:50 or add extra hydration. We cover this in our flour guide.

    How do I store fresh herbs?

    Treat like flowers: trim stems, place in water, cover loosely with a bag. Most herbs last 5‑7 days.

    Why do my cookies spread too much?

    Chill the dough, use butter at cool room temp, and check your oven temperature. We have a full troubleshooting table.
