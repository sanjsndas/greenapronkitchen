Cookies Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience, remember preferences, and understand how you interact with Green Apron Kitchen. This policy explains what we collect and how you can manage your choices.

🍪 Our approach is simple: we only use essential and functional cookies to keep the site running smoothly. No intrusive tracking or third-party data selling. You’re in control.

Types of cookies we use

Name Purpose Duration Type
session_id Maintains your session while you browse Session essential
pref_theme Remembers your display preferences (light/dark) 1 year functional
ga_visit Anonymous analytics to improve content 2 years analytics
recipe_fav Saves your favorite recipes (local storage) Persistent functional

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third-party networks. Our analytics are privacy‑first and aggregated.

Your cookie preferences — you can update or withdraw consent anytime.

How we use cookie data

We collect minimal data to ensure the site works correctly and to understand which recipes and articles are most popular. All data is anonymized and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Essential: necessary for basic functionality (login, navigation).
  • Functional: remember your preferences, like theme or saved recipes.
  • Analytics: aggregate usage statistics to help us improve content.

Manage your cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, some features may not work as expected. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best experience.

🔒 Privacy first: We never sell or misuse your data. For more, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Privacy.

Quick cookie FAQ

Do you use third-party cookies? No, we only use first-party cookies.
Can I opt out? Yes — use your browser settings or our preference buttons above.
How often do you update this policy? We review it yearly, or when regulations change.
What about data from forms? That’s covered in our privacy policy.
